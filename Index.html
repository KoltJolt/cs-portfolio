Steam

Steam is one of my fun applications that I have downloaded. Steam is basically a place where you can buy games, play them and make friends/enemies.This software will help you play games on your computer a lot better than just downloading a game from a website. Also with the steam interface, you can hit shift+tab to show your in-game browser, friends, group notifications and more! Your steam level is also a thing to talk about, your steam level showd what you have collected (steam cards, free game tickets, coupons, etc.)How ever you have to get all the trading cards from the games you play, by either trading other players or playing and hoping you randomly find one in your steam inbox. If you don't have money, no problem, steam has a lot of free to play games for you too enjoy, but note some of them might have micro transactions. There are a wide array of genres for you to play like, fps, rts, rpg, and roleplay.
